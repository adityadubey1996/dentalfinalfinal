About Course

(MANAGEMENT OF IMPACTED 3RD MOLARS) • Introduction and classification • Causes of impacted teeth • Indications and contraindications for removal • Complications of impacted tooth • Pre-operative evaluation • Radiographic analysis • Surgical Anatomy • Surgical procedure • Surgical Complications and management • Newer Advancements in surgery

CLINICAL CASES (LIVE SURGERY) Surgical management of Horizontal/Distoangular impacted mandibular third molar. Hands-on: Incision and suturing technique

(EXODONTIA, MIONR ORAL SURGERY & EMERGENCIES) • Complex Extractions • Transalveolar Extractions (Open method) • Management of grossly decayed crown (1 / 2 walls missing) • Root stump removal • Sectioning of Regular tooth – Which, when & why..? • Emergencies in Extraction • Uncontrolled Bleeding from socket • Muco-periosteal tear • Oro-antral Fistula • Displacement of tooth into sinus / spaces / aspiration • Dislocation of TMJ • Apicoectomy & Alveoloplasty • Management of Medical Emergencies • Drugs Administration – CPR Demonstration • LIVE SURGERY: Transalveolar Extraction

Requirements

  • Participation limited to a maximum of 6 doctors, confirm your participation at the earliest
  • The Course will comprise of lecture and model Hands on followed by participants getting to work on patients under the supervision of course co-ordinators.
  • Each Participant will be allowed to observe the working of a private practice as part of clinical staff and will be allowed to work on patients to gain confidence.
  • All the materials will be provided by Dental Connect without any additional price.
  • Participants need to bring an Airoter hand piece and Apron.
